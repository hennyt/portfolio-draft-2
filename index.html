About

Hello! My name is Henny Tasker and I am a Master's student at the University of Michigan concentrating in UX Research and Design, with a secondary focus on Human-Computer Interaction. I love learning about people's passions! What makes a person excited about new technology and ways of doing things? What makes a person dread change? What helps people get things done online?

I hold degrees in History, Museum Studies, and New Media Writing. My career started in the Philippines, where I taught English and computer literacy as a Peace Corps Volunteer.

Back stateside, I worked as an Instructional Designer in Higher Education. These experiences have left me with a desire to explore diverse online learning environments and to design accessible computer interactions for everyone.
